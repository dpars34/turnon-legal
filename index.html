Getting Started

SwitchOn is a simple way to share your mood and availability with friends in real-time. Here's how to get started:

  1. Create an account: Sign up using your email or Apple ID
  2. Add friends: Use QR codes or friend codes to connect with people
  3. Turn ON: Flip the switch when you're available and in a good mood
  4. Share your vibe: Add custom statuses with emoji, descriptions, and photos
  5. Stay connected: See when friends are ON and interact with their statuses

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add friends?

A: Tap the "Add Friend" button (person with plus icon) on the main screen. You can add friends by scanning their QR code, showing them your QR code, or entering their friend code manually.

Q: How do I organize my friends into groups?

A: Go to the Friends tab, tap "Manage Groups" at the top, and create custom groups like "Work," "Close Friends," or "Family." You can then add friends to these groups and control who sees when you're ON.

Q: Can I control who sees my status?

A: Yes! On the main screen, tap "Share With" to choose which friend groups can see when you're ON. By default, all friends can see your status.

Q: How do I schedule auto-off?

A: When you're ON, tap the "Schedule auto-off" button below the main toggle. You can choose quick options (30 minutes, 1 hour, etc.) or set a specific time.

Q: How do I add photos to my status update?

A: In the "Update" section on the main screen, tap the photo icon to add an image from your camera or photo library. You can also add photos to comments on friends' statuses.

Q: How do I create custom status emoji?

A: Tap the status dropdown, then "Add Custom" at the bottom. Choose an emoji and add a short description (up to 20 characters). Your custom statuses will be saved for quick access.

Q: Can I block someone?

A: Yes. In the Friends tab, tap on a friend to view their details, then tap "Block User." Blocked users won't be able to see your status or interact with you.

Q: How do I turn off notifications?

A: Go to the Profile tab, tap the settings icon, then "Notifications." You can disable specific notification types or turn them off completely in your device settings.

Account & Privacy

Deleting Your Account

Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

To delete your account:

  1. Go to the Profile tab
  2. Tap the settings icon (gear)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Enter your password to confirm
  5. Tap "Delete Account" to permanently remove your data

When you delete your account, we will:

  • Delete your personal information and profile data
  • Remove all your status updates, comments, and likes
  • Delete all images you've uploaded
  • Remove you from all friends' friend lists
  • Delete your Firebase authentication account

Privacy & Data Protection

Your privacy is important to us. We only share your status and updates with friends you've explicitly added. You control exactly who can see when you're ON by using friend groups.

We use Firebase (Google) for secure authentication and data storage. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Troubleshooting

Q: My status isn't updating for friends

A: Make sure you have a stable internet connection. Try turning your status OFF and back ON. If the problem persists, try logging out and back in.

Q: I'm not receiving notifications

A: Check that notifications are enabled in your device settings for SwitchOn. Go to Settings > SwitchOn > Notifications and ensure they're turned on. Also check the notification settings within the app.

Q: The app says I'm offline

A: Check your internet connection (WiFi or cellular data). The app requires an active internet connection to sync status updates with friends.

Q: QR code scanner isn't working

A: Make sure you've granted camera permissions to SwitchOn in your device settings. Go to Settings > SwitchOn > Camera and enable access.

Q: Can't upload photos

A: Check that you've granted photo library permissions. Go to Settings > SwitchOn > Photos and select "All Photos" or "Selected Photos."
